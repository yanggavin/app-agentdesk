Common Topics

How do I import data?

AgentDesk currently supports CSV import and export for customers and properties on the Pro plan.

Why are some names, phone numbers, or addresses hidden?

Privacy masking can hide customer names, phone numbers, and property addresses on list screens only.

Why does the tax estimate differ from another source?

Tax rules can vary by city and change over time. Always verify local policy before using the estimate externally.

Before You Contact Support

  • Include your iPhone model and iOS version.
  • State whether you are using English or Simplified Chinese.
  • Describe the exact screen and steps that caused the issue.
  • If the issue involves CSV, send a redacted sample file if possible.

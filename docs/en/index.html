Technical Support

Support for a China-focused real estate workflow app built around RMB and sqm.

AgentDesk helps you manage customers, properties, showings, mortgage scenarios, and tax estimates in one bilingual iPhone app. This page covers setup, common issues, and the legal links required for App Store submission.

Response target

Within 2 business days.

Supported languages

English and Simplified Chinese.

Current standards

RMB currency, sqm area units, and China-market property fields.

Getting started

  1. Open Settings and choose whether the app should follow the system language.
  2. Add a customer with name, phone, WeChat ID, budget, and preferred area.
  3. Add a property with community, address, sqm, rooms, bathrooms, kitchens, floor, price, and media.
  4. Create a showing to connect the customer and one or more properties.
  5. Use the Mortgage and Tax calculator to prepare estimates before follow-up.

Data and plans

  • CSV import and export currently support customers and properties only.
  • CSV import/export and cross-device sync are Pro features.
  • On the Free plan, the app allows up to 2 visible/editable properties and 10 visible/editable customers.

Privacy behavior

Privacy masking can hide customer names, phone numbers, and property addresses on list screens only.

Detail screens and exported files can still show full values when the workflow needs them.

Common issues

Imported CSV text looks incorrect.

Re-save the file as UTF-8 CSV and keep the column headers aligned with the app template.

Tax results differ from another city or website.

Tax settings are configurable, but local city rules and policies can change. Verify against the latest local regulations before using results externally.

Uploaded media is not visible.

Save the property after selecting the media. The latest build renders saved photo and video previews in the editor, list card, and detail screen.

Before contacting support

  • Include your device model and iOS version.
  • State whether the app is in English or Simplified Chinese.
  • Describe the exact screen and steps that triggered the issue.
  • If the issue involves import or export, attach a redacted sample CSV when possible.

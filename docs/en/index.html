Quick start

  1. Open Settings and choose your language if you do not want to follow the system language.
  2. Add your first customer and property.
  3. Create a showing to connect the customer and property.
  4. Use the Mortgage and Tax calculator tabs to prepare estimates.

CSV import and export

  • AgentDesk currently supports CSV import and export for customers and properties.
  • Use UTF-8 CSV files to avoid encoding issues with Chinese text.
  • Import and export are available on the Pro plan.

Privacy behavior

Privacy masking can hide customer names, phone numbers, and property addresses on list screens only.

Detail screens and exported files may still show the full value when the workflow requires it.

Core workflows

  • Customers: track stage, preferred area, budget, and notes.
  • Properties: store community, area, rooms, status, price, and notes.
  • Showings: schedule visits and record the next follow-up action.
  • Calculators: prepare mortgage and tax estimates for deal conversations.

Troubleshooting

My imported CSV shows the wrong characters.

Re-save the file as UTF-8 CSV and make sure the column headers match the app template.

Tax results look different from another city.

That is expected. Tax calculations can vary by city and policy updates. Verify the latest local rules before using the result externally.

I downgraded from Pro.

On Free, only up to 2 properties and 10 customers remain viewable and editable when you are over limits.

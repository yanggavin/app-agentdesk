Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 10, 2026

1. Information you enter

AgentDesk can store data that you enter into the app, including customer records, property records, showings, notes, calculator values, and app settings.

2. How the data is used

The app uses that data to provide CRM workflows, property tracking, showing scheduling, mortgage calculations, tax calculations, localization preferences, and privacy masking options.

3. Where data is stored

By default, your working data is stored on your device. If you enable optional iCloud sync in a supported configuration, eligible data may be synchronized through your Apple iCloud account.

4. Import and export files

AgentDesk supports CSV import and export for customer and property records. You choose the files you import and the files you export. Exported files are your responsibility once they leave the app.

5. Privacy masking behavior

Privacy masking options in the app are designed for list-screen presentation only. Depending on the workflow, detail screens, created files, or external sharing workflows may still contain the full value.

6. Support communications

If you contact support, we will receive the information you send to us, such as your email address and the contents of your message.

7. Data sharing

We do not sell your business data. We only process support requests that you send to us and any platform-managed services needed for app operation.

8. Security

We use reasonable safeguards in the app and related workflows, but no storage or transmission method is completely secure. You remain responsible for access control on your own devices and exported files.

9. Children

AgentDesk is intended for professional workflow use and is not directed to children under 13.

10. Contact

Privacy requests or questions can be sent to support@agentdesk-app.newtree.tech.

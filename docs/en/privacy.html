Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 3, 2026

1. Information you enter

AgentDesk can store information that you enter into the app, including customer records, property records, showings, notes, calculator values, mortgage scenarios, and app settings.

2. How the data is used

The app uses that information to provide customer management, property tracking, showing scheduling, mortgage and tax calculations, localization preferences, privacy masking, and other workflow features visible in the current release.

3. Where data is stored

By default, working data is stored on your device. If you enable optional sync in a supported Pro configuration, eligible data may also be synchronized through your Apple iCloud account.

4. Import and export files

AgentDesk supports CSV import and export for customer and property records. You choose which files to import. You are responsible for any exported files after they leave the app.

5. Privacy masking behavior

Privacy masking is designed for list-screen presentation only. Depending on the workflow, detail screens, exported files, or sharing workflows may still contain the full value.

6. Purchases and billing services

If you buy or restore AgentDesk Pro, Apple processes the payment and related store transaction. We use RevenueCat to retrieve product availability, entitlement status, purchase state, and customer self-service purchase management features such as restore or Customer Center.

7. App Store privacy summary

For the current release, the purchase-related information processed through Apple and RevenueCat is used for app functionality and purchase analytics. AgentDesk currently uses RevenueCat's app user identifier generated by the SDK and does not use custom account login identifiers, advertising identifiers, or third-party advertising SDKs in the released iPhone build.

8. Optional iCloud sync

If you enable optional iCloud sync, customer records, property records, showing records, mortgage scenarios, settings, and related workflow data may be synchronized through Apple's CloudKit and iCloud services. This sync is optional and can be turned off in Settings.

9. Support communication

If you contact support, we receive the information you send to us, such as your email address and the contents of your message.

10. Data sharing

We do not sell the business data you enter into the app. Data may still pass through Apple platform services when you use optional iCloud-based sync or standard device features. Purchase-related data may also be processed by Apple and RevenueCat to verify your Pro access and manage billing support flows.

11. Security

We use reasonable safeguards in the app and related workflows, but no storage or transmission method is completely secure. You remain responsible for protecting device access, account access, and any exported files.

12. Children

AgentDesk is intended for professional workflow use and is not directed to children under 13.

13. Contact

Privacy questions or requests can be sent to support@agentdesk-app.newtree.tech.
